About

I'm an investigative journalist with a focus on data-driven reporting and government accountability.

My work has been recognized with a Pulitzer Prize for Breaking News Reporting and spans investigations into public finance, election systems, and government transparency. I specialize in extracting stories from complex datasets and public records.

Professional Background

My reporting career has focused on making opaque systems visible to the public—whether that's tracking tax policy changes, analyzing election data, or filing FOIA requests until we get answers.

Approach
